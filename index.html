About the Company Behind Breeze

Breeze Inc. develops a messenger that prioritizes security and privacy above all else.
Our goal is to create a space where everyone can communicate freely and with peace of mind.
We are constantly striving to provide a secure and convenient communication experience that goes beyond simple message delivery.

Our Core Values:

  • Privacy: We protect user data and manage it transparently.
  • Security: We prevent message leaks through strong encryption.
  • Convenience: We provide an intuitive and easy-to-use interface.
  • Reliability: We provide a stable service that you can always count on.
