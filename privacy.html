Effective Date: October 26, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Breeze! This Privacy Policy explains how Breeze Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information when you use our website (the "Website") and our messaging app, Breeze (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Website

  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies (e.g., web beacons, pixels) to collect information about your browsing activity on our Website. This information may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages viewed, and the dates/times of your visits.
  • Contact Form: If you contact us through a form on our Website, we may collect your name, email address, and any other information you choose to provide.

2.2 App

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your username and password.
  • Profile Information: You may choose to provide a profile picture.
  • Messages: Your messages are end-to-end encrypted. This means that only you and the recipient can read them. We cannot access the content of your encrypted messages.
  • Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access the App, such as the device model and operating system.
  • Log Information: We collect log information related to your use of the App, which may include your IP address and usage patterns.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Website and the App.
  • To personalize your experience on the Website and the App.
  • To communicate with you, including responding to your inquiries and providing customer support.
  • To analyze usage patterns and trends to help us understand how our Website and App are used.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security vulnerabilities.

4. Information Sharing

  • We do *not* sell or rent your personal information to third parties.
  • We may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating the Website and the App (e.g., hosting providers, analytics providers). These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.
  • We may disclose your information if required by law or legal process.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. For the App, this includes end-to-end encryption for messages. For the Website, we utilize secure connections (HTTPS) and other security best practices. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Choices

  • Website Cookies: You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies.
  • App Account: You can update your account information within the App at any time. You can also delete your account.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Website and App are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the Website and within the App.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@breeze.cat
